Rules of the Game

This is not Nam, this is bowling, there are rules

-- Walter from The Big Lebowski

Base Play

  • Conquering a Node costs you 1 token
  • If you maintain a node for 30 seconds and press the easy button, you get 5 tokens
  • Freezing another account costs you one token
  • Your goal is to take over all 3 networks

Being Frozen

Freezing doesn't work automatically, You need to hardcode the address in and implement the contract call in the web3.service.ts file.

  • You can not conquer a node while frozen
  • You can not collect tokens while frozen
  • You can freeze other accounts while frozen
  • The freeze only affects the current network. You can switch to other networks to not be frozen.
